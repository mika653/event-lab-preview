Le Sérum
Nocturne

Six years in the making. A night serum that pauses time on the surface of your skin. Hong Kong is the first stop in Asia.

22 November 2026 · The Upper House, Hong Kong · By invitation

№ 27 / 80

The Evening

An invitation, not a launch.

i.

The Product

A 30ml bottle of Le Sérum Nocturne. Hand-blended in the family's atelier in Provence. Each guest receives one — №27 through №80 — signed by founder Camille Langlois.

ii.

The Evening

Champagne. A private viewing of the collection. A short conversation between Camille and Lane Crawford's beauty director, Mei Lin. Then a quiet dinner for those who stay.

iii.

The House

Founded in Aix-en-Provence in 1934 by perfumer Henri Langlois. Now in its third generation. Stocked at Bergdorf Goodman, Lane Crawford, and Selfridges.

Can I bring my own toys to be signed? +

Yes — up to 2 per signing queue. Some artists may restrict to items they sold at the booth.

Is there an age limit? +

All ages welcome. Under 12s enter free with a paying adult.

Will drops sell out? +

Probably. Drop windows are listed on the schedule. Collector Preview pass holders get first access on Saturday.
